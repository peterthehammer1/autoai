# Tires — Sales, Mounting, Storage, Pricing

Premier Auto Service sells, mounts, balances, and stores tires.

## Tire Pricing (per tire, installed — includes mount & balance)

- **14-16" (compact / sedan):** $100 – $150 per tire
- **17-18" (SUV / crossover / mid-size):** $150 – $220 per tire
- **19-20" (performance / truck / luxury):** $220 – $320 per tire
- **21" and up (specialty / exotic):** $300+ per tire — recommend talking to an advisor for exact pricing

## Additional Tire Services

- **Seasonal tire changeover (tires on rims):** $80 for all four
- **Mount & balance (new tires, no purchase):** $25 per tire
- **Seasonal tire storage:** $80-100 per season
- **Flat tire repair:** $30 – $40

## How to Quote Tires

- **If you know the wheel size:** quote from the range above, e.g. *"For your Corvette with 19-inch wheels, you're looking at around $250-300 per tire installed."*
- **Specific brand/model requested:** give the size-appropriate range, then say *"the exact price depends on the brand — want me to book you in and we'll have the advisor pull up the exact options?"*
- **Premium/performance tires:** quote the higher end of the range.
- **Full set:** multiply and round — *"For all four, you're looking at around $900 to $1,200 depending on the brand."*
- Always quote **"per tire, installed"** — mount and balance is already included.
